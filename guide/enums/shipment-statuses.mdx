### Overview

The following enumerated values represent each of the shipment statuses that are supported by the ReturnPilot API:

| ID | Status    | Description                                        |
|----|-----------|----------------------------------------------------|
| 1  | Inactive  | The customer has not returned any parcel(s)        |
| 2  | Pending   | The customer is returning order parcel(s)          |
| 3  | Transit   | The parcel(s) are en-route to their destination    |
| 4  | Delivered | The parcel(s) have arrived at their destination    |
