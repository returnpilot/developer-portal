### Overview

The following enumerated values represent each of the shipment statuses that are supported by the ReturnPilot API:

| ID | Status   | Description                                 |
|----|----------|---------------------------------------------|
| 1  | Pending  | The customer is returning this shipment     |
| 2  | Transit  | The shipment is en-route to its destination |
| 3  | Arrived  | The shipment has arrived at its destination |
| 4  | Refunded | The shipment's label has been refunded      |
