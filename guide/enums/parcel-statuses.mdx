### Overview

The following enumerated values represent each of the parcel statuses that are supported by the ReturnPilot API:

| ID | Status  | Description                               |
|----|---------|-------------------------------------------|
| 1  | Dormant | The customer has not returned this parcel |
| 2  | Pending | The customer is returning this parcel     |
| 3  | Transit | The parcel is en-route to its destination |
| 4  | Arrived | The parcel has arrived at its destination |
