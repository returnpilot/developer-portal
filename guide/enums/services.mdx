### Overview

The following enum values represent the services that are supported by the ReturnPilot API:

| ID | Services  | Description                                                                  |
|----|-----------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1  | Basic     | Expect a minimal service with slower transit and casual handling, but at a generally lower price. |
| 2  | Premium   | Expect a comprehensive service with faster transit and deliberate handling, but at a generally higher price. |
