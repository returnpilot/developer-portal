### Overview

The following enum values represent the shipment statuses that are supported by the ReturnPilot API:

| ID | Shipment Status | Description                                  |
|----|-----------------|----------------------------------------------|
| 1  | Draft           | Shipment is ready for purchase.              |
| 2  | Pending         | Shipment is currently being processed.       |
| 3  | Confirmed       | Shipment is awaiting collection or drop-off. |
| 4  | Dispatched      | Shipment is en-route to its destination.     |
| 5  | Completed       | Shipment has arrived at its destination.     |
